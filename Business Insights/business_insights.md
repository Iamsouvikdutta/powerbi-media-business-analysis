# Key Business Insights

## Print Business Insights

- Net circulation remained above 89%, indicating strong print demand despite digital expansion.
- Return rates averaged around 5%, highlighting opportunities to optimize distribution efficiency.
- Major cities such as Mumbai, Jaipur, and Delhi contributed significantly to overall circulation.

## Advertisement Revenue Insights

- Total advertisement revenue exceeded ₹2.22 billion.
- Commercial brands remained major contributors to advertising revenue.
- Government sector advertising provided stable revenue streams.
- Higher net circulation cities generally generated stronger advertising revenue.

## Digital Adoption Insights

- Overall user engagement stood at 55.41%, indicating moderate digital acceptance.
- Tier 1 cities demonstrated the highest digital engagement levels.
- Mobile App Beta showed the strongest engagement among digital platforms.
- High bounce rates across several platforms indicate opportunities to improve user experience.

## Strategic Recommendations

- Optimize print distribution to reduce return rates.
- Prioritize high-performing cities for premium advertising offerings.
- Invest further in mobile application enhancements.
- Improve platform speed and readability to reduce bounce rates.
- Expand digital initiatives in Tier 1 and Tier 2 markets to accelerate adoption.
