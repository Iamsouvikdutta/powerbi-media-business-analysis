# 📰 Media Business Analysis Dashboard using Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The media industry is experiencing a significant shift from traditional print operations to digital platforms. This project analyzes key business metrics across newspaper circulation, advertisement revenue, and digital adoption to help stakeholders understand business performance and identify opportunities for sustainable growth.

Using Power BI, this dashboard provides actionable insights into print media operations, advertising performance, and digital transformation initiatives, enabling data-driven decision-making for business leaders.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The analysis aims to answer the following business questions:

- How is the print circulation business performing across different markets?
- Which cities contribute the most to newspaper circulation?
- What are the major drivers of advertisement revenue?
- Which advertiser categories generate the highest revenue?
- How successful are digital transformation initiatives?
- How are users engaging with digital platforms?
- What strategic actions can support long-term business growth?

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard development and business storytelling
- **Power Query** – Data transformation and cleaning
- **DAX** – KPI calculations and business measures
- **Data Modeling** – Building relationships between datasets
- **Business Analytics** – Insight generation and strategic recommendations

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### 1️⃣ Executive Overview

**Business Questions:**

> How is the overall media business performing?

**Key Metrics:**

- Total Newspaper Circulation
- Net Circulation %
- Advertisement Revenue
- Digital User Engagement %
- Digital Platform Performance

**Key Insights:**

- Print operations continue to generate stable circulation volumes.
- Advertisement revenue remains a major contributor to business performance.
- Digital initiatives are showing promising user adoption trends.

---

### 2️⃣ Print Circulation Analysis

**Business Questions:**

> Which markets drive newspaper circulation performance?

**Analysis Performed:**

- City-wise circulation analysis
- Net circulation trends
- Return percentage analysis
- Distribution performance monitoring

**Key Insights:**

- Major metropolitan cities contribute significantly to total circulation.
- Return rates indicate opportunities to optimize distribution efficiency.
- Strong circulation performance supports continued advertising opportunities.

---

### 3️⃣ Advertisement Revenue Analysis

**Business Questions:**

> What drives advertisement revenue performance?

**Analysis Performed:**

- Revenue contribution by advertiser category
- City-wise advertisement revenue analysis
- Revenue trend monitoring
- High-value advertiser identification

**Key Insights:**

- Advertisement revenue remains a critical business driver.
- Certain advertiser segments consistently outperform others.
- Markets with higher circulation generally attract stronger advertising demand.

---

### 4️⃣ Detailed Advertisement Performance Analysis

**Business Questions:**

> Which segments present the greatest revenue opportunities?

**Analysis Performed:**

- Category-level revenue analysis
- Revenue concentration assessment
- Growth opportunity identification

**Key Insights:**

- Revenue concentration highlights dependence on key advertiser segments.
- Diversification opportunities exist across emerging categories.
- Strategic focus on high-performing segments can accelerate growth.

---

### 5️⃣ Digital Adoption Analysis

**Business Questions:**

> How effectively are customers adopting digital platforms?

**Analysis Performed:**

- User engagement analysis
- Platform-wise adoption trends
- Bounce rate monitoring
- Customer interaction evaluation

**Key Insights:**

- Digital engagement levels vary significantly across platforms.
- Mobile platforms demonstrate stronger customer adoption.
- Bounce rate patterns indicate opportunities to improve user experience.

---

### 6️⃣ Digital Launch Performance Analysis

**Business Questions:**

> How successful are digital transformation initiatives?

**Analysis Performed:**

- Digital launch performance tracking
- User acquisition monitoring
- Engagement trend evaluation
- Growth opportunity assessment

**Key Insights:**

- Digital initiatives show encouraging early adoption patterns.
- High-performing markets can serve as benchmarks for expansion.
- Continuous optimization is essential to maximize long-term digital growth.

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Print circulation continues to provide a stable foundation for business performance.
- Advertisement revenue remains highly dependent on circulation strength and advertiser mix.
- Mobile-based platforms exhibit stronger user engagement compared to traditional channels.
- Digital transformation initiatives are gaining traction but require ongoing optimization.
- Distribution efficiency improvements can reduce return rates and improve profitability.
- Expanding successful digital strategies into emerging markets presents significant growth opportunities.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

- Optimize newspaper distribution networks to reduce return percentages.
- Focus premium advertising offerings in high-circulation markets.
- Strengthen digital platform experiences to improve user engagement.
- Prioritize investment in high-performing digital initiatives.
- Develop integrated print and digital growth strategies to maximize overall business value.
- Continuously monitor user behavior to support data-driven digital transformation decisions.
